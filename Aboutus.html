OUR COMMUNITIES

TEAM UP FOR A HEALTHY PAKISTAN

Since 2010, we’ve inspired over 250,000 children to stay healthy and active through Team Up for Healthy PAKISTAN, our innovative partnership program focused on childhood health and well-being.

GARDEN GRANTS

Over 250 school gardens have received “Team Up for Healthy School Garden” garden grants. We have inspired over 25,000 kids through our partnership with KidsGardening.org since 2009.

NON-PROFIT FUNDRAISING

Each year, Shrek Juice and our local franchise partners give back over 1 crore rupees to support our local communities. Shrek offers easy fundraising options perfect for your local sports team or organization.
