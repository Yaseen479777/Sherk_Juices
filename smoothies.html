Smoothie

Smoothies are a staple among health junkies, and we have to agree: they're ridiculously easy to make, filled with fruits and veggies, and make you feel like a pro when you whip up a particularly delicious blend.

Cranberry Citrus Smoothie

This citrus drinks provides a burst of refreshing tart flavor from cranberries and oranges.Bananas and a hint of vanilla extract add balance, with a base of plain Greek yogurt for protein and creaminess,feel free to swap those in!

Apple Chrisp Smoothie

Savor the taste of fall with apple cider that's blended with Greek yogurt, rolled oats, pecans, cinnamon, and nutmeg. This delicious smoothie is rich in protein and beta-glucan, a fiber that improves running endurance.

Mango Madness

Take advantage ripe mango's disease-fighting ability with this delicious smoothie recipe.

Pumkin Coconut Smoothie

You can feel good sipping on this rich, creamy smoothie. Only five ingredients are needed to whip up this blend, which works for many diets.This blogger suggests adding a scoop of collagen powder for protein!

Orange Dream Creamicle

Need to cool down after a tough workout or a hot day at the beach? Lap up this low-cal, citrus-infused healthy smoothie recipe.
