Chocolate Shakes

Chocolate is the first luxury. It has so many things wrapped up in it: Deliciusness in the moment, childhood memories, and that grin-inducing feeling of getting a reward for being good.

Chocolate Brownie Shake

Add the chocolate ice cream,brownie and milk to a blender.Pipe the whipped cream using a pastry bag fitted with a star tip and drizzle with some extra chocolate sauce,brownie crumbs,shaved chocolate and a cherry.

Chocolate Chip Shake

In a blender, combine vanilla ice cream, chocolate chip cookies, whole milk, chocolate chips and vanilla extract blend until smooth. Pour into milkshake glasses and serve immediately.

Mango Chocolate Shake

Chocolate and mango, two delicious ingredients and when you combine them you get a delectable dish! Mango Chocolate Milkshake! .Pour the milkshake in a tall serving glass.

Double Fudge Cookie Shake

Combine the milk, ice cream and brownie in a blender and Let the froth settle before pouring into a tall glass. Add a mountain of whipped cream to the top and scatter the flaked chocolate onto the top.

Chocolate Caramel Shake

Add the chocolate and vanilla, stir until smooth. Cool. DRIZZLE 1 tablespoon of chocolate caramel syrup into each glass. Combine the remaining syrup, milk and ice cream into a blender and blend until frothy.
