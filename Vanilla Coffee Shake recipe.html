Protein Shakes

Get lean and strong with the ultimate list of protein shakes. These recipes enhance your protein power with the addition of healthy, delicious ingredients.

Vanilla Coffee Shake

Replace your sugar-filled blended drink from the coffee shop with this healthier, protein-packed version.Trust us on this one.

Recipe

  1. Blend the coffee, vanilla ice cream and 1 tablespoon of chocolate syrup together in a blender until smooth.
  2. Pour the milkshake into one very large or two medium sized glasses.
  3. Top with a very generous head of whipped cream and drizzle with additional chocolate syrup.
  4. Serve immediately.

Health Benefits

  1. rich in plant-based proteins
  2. low-fat
  3. rich in plant-based proteins
  4. contains NO refined sugar
  5. Helps in weight loss
  6. rich in potassium, calcium, and iron
