Recipe

  1. Wash, peel and cut the mango into pieces.
  2. In a blender, combine the mango cut into pieces, yoghurt, honey, chocolate powder, sugar and milk until obtain a very creamy mixture.
  3. Whip the cream together with the powdered sugar and the vanilla in a electric mixer until stiff peaks form (donâ€™t overbeat the cream).
