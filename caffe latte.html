Caffe Latte

A caffè latte is simply a shot or two of bold, tasty espresso with fresh, sweet steamed milk over it. Some prefer to add syrup or extra espresso to the recipe. Some maintain that it is entirely perfect as is.

Caramel Cinnamon Latte

Caramelly sweet sauce and fragrant cinnamon enhance the richness of our signature espresso, with a hint of warm spice in every sip. and cookie crumbles for that extra crunch - irresistible, iced or blended!

Pumpkin Spice Latte

IT'S BACK! Sweet yet spicy flavours of creamy pumpkin, cinnamon, nutmeg and clove pair perfectly with our bold espresso to create this one-of-a-kind Halloween hit. MMMWAHAHAH!

Chai Tea Latte

The warm, aromatic flavours of chai tea have their roots in the ancient Ayurvedic tradition of Pakistan. Featuring ingredients gathered from around the globe.

Peanut Butter Pumpkin Spice Latte

When nights start to cool down and fall feels like it’s in the air, many of us look forward to our first pumpkin spice latte of the season. I love this seasonal rite of passage.
