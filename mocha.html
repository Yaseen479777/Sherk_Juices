White Chocolate Mocha

Fans of white chocolate will tell you that they love it for its elegant, creamy taste. That its smooth subtlety makes it a perfect complement to bolder flavours. Well, full-bodied espresso.

Caramel mocha

A smooth, buttery drizzle of caramel adds so much to this classic warmer. Enjoy the richness of the classic mocha, and finish with a drizzle of caramel syrup for extra sweetness and warmth.

Caffe Mocha
