Strawberry Savior Shake

Frozen strawberries minimize prep time and maximize nutrients in the most indulgent way to take your creatine.Trust us on this one.

Recipe

  1. Wash the strawberries and pat dry them. Remove stems and cut into halves.
  2. Add milk, ice cream and halved strawberries in a blender jar. If you like, you can add 1 teaspoon sugar or honey for extra sweetness.
  3. Blend until smooth puree. Pour it into serving glasses

Health Benefits

  1. Keeps the heart healthy.
  2. Highly source of protein.
  3. Regulate Blood Sugar Levels
  4. Balanced blood presure.
  5. Improve Vision Health
  6. Improve Brain Health
