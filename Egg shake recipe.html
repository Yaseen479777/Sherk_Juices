Egg Cellent Shake

Try adding a little nutmeg to this decadently custard-like shake, and enjoy bulking season.Trust us on this one.

Recipe

  1. In a blender, combine ice cream, milk of your choice, and Cadbury Mini Eggs and blend until smooth.
  2. Pour milkshake into two glasses and stir 1 Tbsp each of caramel sauce and chocolate sauce into each glass.
  3. Top milkshakes with Cadbury Creme Eggs and sprinkles, if desired.
